Brand Identity

Logo

The dragon symbolizes strength, resilience, and auspicious spirit—aligned with fitness goals of building a strong body and a determined mindset.

The S-shaped streamline echoes the dynamic trajectory of human movement; the wrapped lines resemble muscle texture and the gathering of power, representing shaping body lines and building muscle strength.

The hidden “9”/“S” suggests longevity and excellence (9) or Strength/Shape (S), expressing our belief in pursuing peak results through long-term consistency.

Color

We use black to represent power, professionalism, and stability—matching the fitness industry’s focus on strength and expertise.

Brand meaning

  1. Value: Using the dragon as the core symbol, we encourage users to build a dragon-like body and spirit through training—motivating the pursuit of health and strength.
  2. Differentiation: We combine a traditional dragon totem with modern athletic motion, strengthening distinctiveness and recall versus typical gym visuals.
  3. Emotional resonance: The dragon evokes “breakthrough and surpassing limits,” aligning with the core fitness experience of challenging oneself.
